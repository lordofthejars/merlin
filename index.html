The first big Java and JVM conference from Spain

JBCNConf will be the first big Java and JVM ecosystem conference from Spain.

2 full days to share knowledge, experiences, meet enthusiasts and geeks to learn about new technologies.

Barcelona will host this great event next 26th and 27th of June organized by the Barcelona Java Users Group.

We are proud of celebrating this conference in our great city. As you may know, Barcelona is one of the best cities in the world to work in, with an interesting start-up ecosystem and a lot of communities about software around and also, we have a beautiful city with a lot of things to see and one of the best city beaches in the world!

Do not miss this great opportunity to participate in this big event, to immerse yourself in 2 days with interesting talks about Java, the JVM and the opensource world.
